Instructions:

This self-assessment will help you identify your likes and dislikes as well as your strengths. Listed below in the blue column are different career fields of interest. Below each field are the jobs that are often associated with that path. In order to determine which field you might be interested in, you should rate each one. If you don't know what a particular field is, hover your cursor over them, and an explanation will appear.
1. Complete the YELLOW column by selecting the choice that corresponds to your answer. You have 3 choices:
  • No = You have NO interest in the job in the left column
  • Maybe = You MIGHT have an interest or a SLIGHT interest
  • Yes = You ARE interested in the job from the first column
2. If your answer from the YELLOW column was "maybe" or "yes," then complete the GREEN column. When completing the GREEN column, make the following choices:
  • Items = if you are interested in or like working with ITEMS or THINGS
  • Interpersonal = if you like working with PEOPLE or INTERPERSONAL related tasks
  • Ideas/Info = if you like or are interested in working with ITEMS, INTERPERSONAL, or IDEAS/INFO
It is also important to remember that when you are selecting an interest or like, related to 'Items', 'Interpersonal' or 'Ideas/Info', that you consider it in relation to the job in the first column. For example - under 'General Business', let's say you chose 'Yes' in the YELLOW column for 'Sales.' That means your next step is to select a choice in the GREEN column and decide if you would most like to work with 'Items' or 'Interpersonal' or 'Ideas/Info' in a 'Sales' related job. If you would like to add a topic for 'Other' - enter your topic and select a YELLOW and or GREEN value as well. Keep working through the blue column in the same way.
