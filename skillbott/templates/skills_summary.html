Get into a group of three students to discuss your answers to the following questions-then share with the entire class in a wrap up discussion.
  1. Share your skills scores with your group members. What were the scores of your 5 categories for the YELLOW section (Current Skills)? What were the scores of your 5 categories for the GREEN section (Future Skills)?
  2. Does your list have any "surprise" skills? Were there any skills you identified that you had never seriously considered or knew you wanted to learn? If yes, which ones? If no, why not?
  3. How well do you feel your list represents your skills in each category? Do you possess additional strong skills in each category that might not have been represented on the inventory? If yes, mention what they are and add them to the "MY ASSESSMENT TABLE".
  4. Review your "MY ASSESSMENT TABLE." Look at the first line and read it across from left to right. In the example on the previous page, it reads: "General Business-Items-Medium Current Skills-High Desire for Future Training." Read your 5 lines to your group in the same manner. Then answer the following:
  • a. Do any of your lines contain "High Current Skills and Low Desire for Future Training"? If so, what might that indicate? Might it indicate that your lack of desire to sharpen those skills could be due to a lack of interest? Or do you feel you have enough training? Discuss it with the group and be sure to follow up with your teacher/counselor to figure out its meaning.
  • b. Look back at the skills in one of your categories. How might the skills in the list fit or be impacted by your interest to work with IDEAS, INTERPERSONAL or ITEMS?
  • c. With your counselor/teacher be sure to review all of your highest skills and in ALL of your categories and think about how they link with your desire to work with IDEAS, ITEMS or INTERPERSONAL.
